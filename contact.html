Common questions about our mentorship and coaching model.

How does the mentorship model work?
We assign a dedicated senior mentor who monitors your daily progress, analyzes your mock test results, and creates a weekly customized study plan based on your speed and accuracy.
Is both online and offline support available?
Yes, we have high-tech classrooms in Kota for offline students, and our elite virtual mentorship platform ensures that students from anywhere in India get the same level of intensity and support.
Can Droppers join middle of the session?
Yes, we have specialized 'Fast-Track' modules for droppers designed to cover the high-weightage syllabus quickly and jump directly into intensive testing.
How do I get the Narayana/Chaitanya test series?
We provide full administrative access to premium South-India based test series as part of our integration package. Contact us for the enrollment steps.
