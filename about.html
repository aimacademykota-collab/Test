The Visionary Leader

Govind Singh Hada

Chief Strategist and Maths Mentor

Govind Singh Hada has been a prominent figure in the Kota education industry for over a decade. Realizing that raw talent often goes misguided in massive overcrowded coaching batches, he established Aim Academy.


His core philosophy revolves around Precision Mentorship. Every student requires a distinct approach based on their analytical capacity and grasping speed.


Over the years, he has successfully mentored top-tier IIT JEE rankers, shaping their logical thinking specifically suited for high-pressure examination environments.
